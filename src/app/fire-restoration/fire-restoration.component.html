Fire Restoration

Fires can be devastating, leaving behind a trail of destruction and despair. At First Class, we specialize in expert fire restoration services that go beyond rebuilding spaces. We are dedicated to ensuring safety, comfort, and a fresh start for our clients after the trauma of a fire disaster.

Our fire restoration process is designed to address every aspect of recovery, from assessing the initial damage to the final touches of rebuilding. Here's how we achieve this with expert care:

  • Assessment and Inspection: Our certified fire restoration experts conduct a thorough assessment to determine the extent of fire, smoke, and water damage. This assessment guides our restoration plan.
  • Secure and Stabilize: We employ specialized techniques and equipment to remove smoke residues and soot from walls, ceilings, and surfaces.
  • Smoke and Soot Removal: We employ industrial-grade drying and dehumidification equipment to eliminate moisture from affected areas, preventing mold growth.
  • Cleaning and Sanitization:We clean and sanitize all affected areas, including personal belongings, to ensure a safe and healthy environment.
